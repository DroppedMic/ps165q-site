Academic Programs

Programs that set our students apart

Gifted & Talented
G&T Program
An accelerated curriculum for students identified through the NYC DOE gifted and talented admissions process. Emphasizes advanced problem-solving, independent research, and enriched content across all core subjects.
Learn more
Enrichment
NEST Program
Nurturing Excellence through STEM and the arts, the NEST program provides project-based learning with integrated technology experiences.
Wellbeing
Social-Emotional Learning
Structured SEL curriculum building self-awareness, empathy, and responsible decision-making across every grade level.
Creative Arts
The Arts
Visual arts, music, and theater programs including annual productions — like our celebrated Shrek performance — that build confidence and creativity.
Athletics
Sports
Competitive and recreational athletics developing teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness in our Shooting Stars athletes.
Academic Support
Literacy & Math Intervention
Targeted academic support through small-group instruction and one-on-one tutoring to ensure every student meets or exceeds grade-level standards in reading and mathematics.
Technology
Digital Learning
1:1 device integration, coding curriculum, and digital citizenship instruction preparing students for a technology-driven future with responsible, creative computer use.
