Our Mission

Cultivating citizens of the world and life-long learners

We build a solid and equitable foundation through critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication — preparing every student to thrive in a diverse and interconnected world.

Academic Programs

Programs that set our Shooting Stars apart

Gifted & Talented
G&T Program
An accelerated curriculum for students identified through the NYC DOE admissions process. Emphasizes advanced problem-solving, independent research, and enriched content across all core subjects.
Learn more
Enrichment
NEST Program
Nurturing Excellence through STEM and the arts. Project-based learning with integrated technology experiences for deeper engagement.
Wellbeing
Social-Emotional Learning
Building self-awareness, empathy, and responsible decision-making across every grade level.
Creative Arts
The Arts
Visual arts, music, and theater — including our celebrated annual productions that build confidence and creativity.
Athletics
Sports
Competitive and recreational athletics developing teamwork, discipline, and physical fitness.
Academic Support
Literacy & Math Intervention
Targeted small-group instruction and one-on-one tutoring to ensure every student meets or exceeds grade-level standards in reading and mathematics.
Technology
Digital Learning
1:1 device integration, coding curriculum, and digital citizenship preparing students for a technology-driven future.
