"Every child deserves an equitable, world-class education that celebrates their unique gifts and prepares them to lead."

As Principal of P.S. 165Q for the past decade, I am honored to lead this incredible school community. Our mission is simple yet profound: cultivate a solid and equitable foundation for every student to become a citizen of the world and a lifelong learner through critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication.

With the dedicated support of Assistant Principals Andrea L. Luongo and Marianna H. Werth, our exceptional staff, and our engaged families, we are building a school culture where every learner thrives.

TDN

Tiffany L. Davis-Nealy

Principal, P.S. 165Q
