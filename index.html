A Message from
Our Principal

TD

Flushing

Tiffany L. Davis-Nealy

Principal, P.S. 165Q
At P.S. 165Q, we believe every child is a Shooting Star — bright, unique, and capable of extraordinary things.

Welcome to our school community. For over six decades, P.S. 165Q has been a cornerstone of Flushing, Queens — nurturing young minds from Pre-K through Grade 5. Our dedicated staff works every day to build a solid, equitable foundation through critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication. We are proud of our Gifted & Talented program, our NEST enrichment track, and the wide range of arts, athletics, and social-emotional learning opportunities we offer. We look forward to partnering with you on your child's educational journey.

Cultivating citizens of the world and life-long learners

We build a solid and equitable foundation through critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication — preparing every student to thrive in a diverse, interconnected world.
